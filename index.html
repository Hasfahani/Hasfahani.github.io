Arcade duels vs CPU
simple to learn, intense to master

You fly the yellow ship. The computer commands the red. Pick a difficulty, weave through bullets, grab power-ups, and land the final hit. Fast restarts and crisp feedback keep you in the flow.

  • Gameplay
  • Fast

Solo vs. CPU with reactive AI

Movement patterns and fire chance scale by difficulty, keeping Easy chill, Medium balanced, and Hard sweaty. Bounce-aware navigation avoids edges and keeps the chase alive.

Power-ups change momentum

Random health power-ups spawn during rounds. Collide to collect and swing the duel back in your favor. Clean HUD shows health and score at a glance.

Juice: music, hits, quick restarts

Menu music, firing and hit sounds, pause/resume, and a bold win screen with instant replay prompt. Get back into action in seconds.

Features

Lean, responsive, and readable. Built with Python & Pygame.

  • Three Difficulties

    Easy / Medium / Hard tune AI speed and fire chance for any mood.

  • 60 FPS Feel

    Snappy controls and crisp feedback keep dogfights tight and fair.

  • Health & Score HUD

    Track the duel in real time. First to drop the opponent to 0 wins.

  • Power-ups

    Occasional health boosts reward map awareness and timing.

  • Pause / Resume

    Hit Esc any time. Quick restarts after the win screen.

  • Simple Controls

    Move: W/A/S/D  •  Fire: Enter  •  Pause: Esc

Play it now

Download the project, drop the PNG/MP3 assets next to main.py, and run: python main.py. Built by Habib Asfahani.
